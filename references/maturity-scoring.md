# Content Maturity Scoring

## 4-Axis Quality Rubric (0-100 total)

Each article is scored on 4 axes, each worth 0-25 points.

### Axis 1: SEO Quality (0-25)

| Criteria | Points | Measurement |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Keyword in Title (H1) | 5 | Main keyword present in title |
| Keyword in H2 headings | 5 | At least 2 H2s contain main keyword |
| Keyword density | 5 | 1-2% density (distributed across H2, H3, body, FAQ) |
| Meta description optimization | 5 | 120-160 chars, keyword in first 60 chars |
| URL slug quality | 3 | ≤6 words, keyword-rich, clean |
| Heading structure | 2 | Proper H2/H3 hierarchy, no H1 in body |

**Passing**: ≥ 20/25

### Axis 2: E-E-A-T Compliance (0-25)

| Criteria | Points | Measurement |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| First-person usage | 6 | 15-20+ occurrences of I/my/we/our |
| Experience evidence | 6 | Specific testing timeline, scenarios, observations |
| Balanced perspective | 5 | 3-5 pros AND 2-3 cons, honest limitations |
| Transparency | 4 | Disclosures, no superlatives, factual language |
| Competitor comparison | 4 | Named real competitors, fair analysis |

**Passing**: ≥ 20/25

### Axis 3: Content Depth (0-25)

| Criteria | Points | Measurement |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Word count | 5 | Sufficient depth (1500+ words typical) |
| FAQ coverage | 5 | At least 6 natural questions |
| Technical detail | 5 | Specifications, parameters, step-by-step |
| Template compliance | 5 | Follows assigned template structure |
| Section completeness | 5 | All required sections present and substantive |

**Passing**: ≥ 20/25

### Axis 4: Reference Authority (0-25)

| Criteria | Points | Measurement |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Reference count | 5 | At least 4-6 authoritative sources |
| URL validity | 5 | All URLs verified (200 status) |
| Domain credibility | 5 | Sources from approved trusted domains |
| Format correctness | 5 | All references use proper `<a href>` format |
| Relevance | 5 | Sources directly relevant to article topic |

**Passing**: ≥ 20/25

## Total Score Interpretation

| Score | Status | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 100 | Perfect | Publish immediately |
| 90-99 | Excellent | Publish with minor fixes |
| 80-89 | Good | Optimize and re-score |
| 70-79 | Fair | Significant optimization needed |
| < 70 | Poor | Major rewrite recommended |

## Convergence

The pipeline iterates until:
- Total score ≥ 90/100, OR
- 3 optimization iterations completed (publish best version)

## Scoring Process

1. **Independent scoring**: Each axis scored independently
2. **Evidence-based**: Every score backed by measurable criteria
3. **Improvement suggestions**: Each sub-score below target gets specific fix recommendations
4. **Before/After tracking**: Track score changes across iterations
